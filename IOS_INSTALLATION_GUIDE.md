## ğŸ”§ **Troubleshooting:**

### **Issue: "Add to Home Screen" not showing**
- **Solution:** Make sure you're using Safari (not Chrome or Firefox)
- In Safari, the option is in the Share menu (square with up arrow)

### **Issue: App doesn't load offline**
- **Solution:** First time you open the app, make sure you're online
- Service worker will cache data for offline use after first load

### **Issue: Bottom tabs are cut off by home indicator**
- **Solution:** Already handled! Safe area insets are applied automatically
- The tabs sit above the home indicator on all iPhones

### **Issue: Pitch view is too small on mobile**
- **Solution:** Use pinch-to-zoom gesture to zoom in on the pitch
- Tap players to select them instead of drag-and-drop
